About

I am passionate about Data Science, Machine Learning, and Data Visualization. I am currently working on a 100 days of code challenge producing and sharing work related to data science ever day for 100 days. I am also on the organizing committee for hackseq, a genomics hackathon in October.

Previously I worked on the classification of tissue types using neural networks, the modelling of epigenetic states in the human thyroid, and literature-based knowledge discovery in biomedical text.

I completed a MSc in Computational Biology at the University of British Columbia and worked as a Bioinformatics Research Assistant at the BC Cancer Agency for a year before starting my Master's. I am co-authored in 3 publications in the general field of Bioinformatics with 1 more publication accepted and 2 more in review.

I volunteered to help out at Software Carpentry workshops and am always interested in new opportunities. Please reach me by twitter @celiassiu.
